# AGENTS.md

## ClassPulse contributor guidance

- Keep this application grounded as an internal school operations tool, not generic SaaS UI.
- Use plain, operational language. Avoid startup or marketing phrasing.
- Preserve restricted student sections and keep student-identifiable information clearly marked.
- Keep data-quality limitations visible in dashboards and notes.
- Prefer clarity and maintainability over decorative polish.
- Keep static data centralized in `src/data/dashboardData.js` unless there is a strong reason to split.
- Preserve the separation between theme tokens, shared components, and views.
